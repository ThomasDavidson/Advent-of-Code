px + py = na(ax+ay) + nb(bx + by)

px: prize X
py: prize Y
na: number of a presses
nb: number of b presses
ax: x movment per a press
ay: y movment per a press
bx: x movment per b press
by: y movment per b press

px = na * ax + nb * bx
na = (px - nb * bx)/ax

py = na * ay + nb * by


py = (px - nb * bx)/ax * ay + nb * by

py = px/ax - nb*bx*ay/ax +nb *by
py = px/ax - nb(bx*ay/ax + by)

py = px/ax - nb(bx*ay/ax + by)

nb = (px/ax - py) / (bx*ay/ax + by)
